STREET LIGHT AUTOMATION

Developed an image processing model that identifies human presence and automatically changes the brightness of street lights around people. This data is then utilised to locate neighbouring street lights and adjust their brightness. Using image processing, the model recognizes and localizes the presence of individuals by characterising their movements, size, and so on. It employs dilation and erosion techniques to gain a closer look at the recognized item.

LOAN ELIGIBILITY PREDICTION

This project was completed with an accuracy of 80% utilising Machine Learning algorithms and Linear Regression.
